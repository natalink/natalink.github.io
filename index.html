Short Bio

I obtained my undergrad degree in Theoretical and Applied Linguistics at the Russian State University for Humanities. After a short internship in a software company ABBYY(annotations for machine translation system), I started PH.D studies at the Institute of Formal and Applied linguistics(UFAL), Charles University in Prague, focusing on analysis of Machine Translation between related languages. Already during my studies and after the graduation I worked as a research assistant on the project relating to text analysis, performing mostly format convertion, parsing, machine learning experiments. At 2017 I received a 2-year postdoc position at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, where I applied my previous knowledge of machine learning and enchanced my skills experimenting with Deep Learning approaches, working on various NLP tasks sentiment analysis, biomedical data processing, related languages etc.
